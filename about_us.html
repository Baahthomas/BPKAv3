Who We Are

More Than a Church: A Kingdom Family

Kingdom Alignment Global (KAG) is a prophetic and apostolic movement raised to cultivate divine alignment in people, ministries, and communities. We exist to host God’s presence, align His people with His purpose, and demonstrate His Kingdom through worship, teaching, prophetic impartation, and apostolic equipping.

KAG is not confined to a building; it is a living, mobile expression of God’s Kingdom. We are sent to illuminate lives, realign hearts, and restore spiritual order wherever God places us.

"You are the light of the world. A city that is set on a hill cannot be hidden." — Matthew 5:14
Vision Statement

Raising Kingdom
Aligners

To raise and release believers who host God's presence, align with divine order, and establish His Kingdom across nations.

Short Form To align lives, communities, and nations with Heaven's purpose.
Mission Statement

We are divinely
mandated to:

  • Host God's presence through worship, teaching, and intercession
  • Align believers to discern seasons and walk in Kingdom authority
  • Create environments for supernatural encounters that produce transformation
  • Establish alignment hubs that restore spiritual order
  • Advance Heaven's culture across generations and nations
Scripture "As you go, preach, saying, 'The kingdom of heaven is at hand.' Heal the sick, cleanse the lepers, raise the dead, cast out demons." — Matthew 10:7–8

Our Core Mandate

The four dimensions that define who we are, what we carry, and where we are going.

Dimension 01
Kingdom
Manifesting God's Government on Earth

The Kingdom is God's rule and culture made visible through surrendered lives. We are advancing God's government through transformed lives in every sphere.

Scripture "The kingdom of God is within you." Luke 17:21
"…righteousness, peace, and joy in the Holy Spirit." Romans 14:17
Dimension 02
Alignment
Restoring Divine Order

Alignment precedes assignment. We bring clarity where chaos has taken root, awakening believers from passivity and restoring vision where religion or culture has obscured God's purposes.

Scripture "Arise, shine; for your light has come!" Isaiah 60:1
"Walk as children of light." Ephesians 5:8
Dimension 03
Church
Carriers of Presence and Power

We are the Ekklesia, not a building, but a community of vessels carrying God's presence. We release glory and manifest Heaven in our gatherings and in every life we touch.

Scripture "The Spirit of the Lord God is upon Me…" Isaiah 61:1–3
"…we have this treasure in earthen vessels…" 2 Cor. 4:7
Dimension 04
Global
A Translocal Apostolic Network

The call is not limited to one city. KAG functions as a network of aligned leaders, fellowships, and hubs raising Kingdom Aligners across church, governance, education, media, and culture.

Scripture "Go into all the world…" Mark 16:15
"Ask of Me, and I will give You the nations…" Psalm 2:8
Discipleship Pathway

From Encounter to Multiplication

A structured five-stage journey that moves every believer from first encounter to raising the next generation of Kingdom leaders.

01
Entry

Believer joins and is introduced to the culture and mission of KAG.

02
Alignment

Learns submission, unity, and understanding of Kingdom order.

03
Activation

Spiritual gifts identified, training received, ministry exposure begins.

04
Assignment

Member released to spheres of influence beyond the church walls.

05
Multiplication

Trained members mentor and raise the next generation of Kingdom leaders.
