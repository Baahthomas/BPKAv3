Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our events and how to attend.

Are Kingdom Alignment Global events open to everyone?
expand_more
Yes. All our events are open to everyone regardless of denomination, background, or experience in the faith. You are welcome just as you are. Simply register and show up ready to encounter God.
How do I receive my registration confirmation?
expand_more
Once you complete registration, a confirmation email will be sent to the address you provided. Please check your spam folder if you do not receive it within a few minutes. You can also contact us via WhatsApp if you need assistance.
Can I attend events online if I'm not in Ghana?
expand_more
Absolutely. Most of our major events are live-streamed on YouTube at no cost. Some events are held exclusively online via Zoom, which are open to attendees worldwide. Register to receive the link and access details.
Will event recordings be made available after?
expand_more
Yes. Recordings of most sessions are made available on our Sportify podcast channel and YouTube page within a few days of the event. Registered attendees may also receive exclusive access to post-event notes and resources.
How can my church or group partner with or host a Kingdom Alignment Global event?
expand_more
We are open to partnerships and hosting requests from churches and ministries across Ghana and beyond. Please reach out via our Contact page or email us at info@kingdombearer.com and our team will be in touch to discuss how we can collaborate.
