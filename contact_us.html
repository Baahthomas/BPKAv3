Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive. Can't find what you need? Send us a message above.

What is Kingdom Alignment Global and what do you stand for?
expand_more
Kingdom Alignment Global (KAG) is a prophetic and apostolic movement raised to cultivate divine alignment in people, ministries, and communities. We exist to host God's presence, align His people with His purpose, and demonstrate His Kingdom through worship, teaching, prophetic impartation, and apostolic equipping. Our tagline says it best: Carrying Heaven's Presence. Establishing Kingdom Order.
What is the Discipleship Pathway and how does it work?
expand_more
Our discipleship pathway is a structured journey: Entry, Alignment, Activation, Assignment, and Multiplication. You begin by joining and learning the KAG culture, grow into understanding Kingdom order, have your gifts identified and activated, get released into your sphere of influence, and eventually mentor others. No one is left behind and no one is left stagnant.
Where can I listen to sermons and worship music?
expand_more
Our sermons are available on Spotify, and YouTube. Our original worship music under B.P.K.A is on Spotify, Boomplay, Audiomack, and YouTube Music. Visit our website to find direct links to all platforms.
How do I give or partner with the ministry?
expand_more
You can sow into the ministry through our Give page on the website. Your support goes toward worship and sound production, discipleship resources, apostolic outreach, compassion projects, and establishing Kingdom Alignment Hubs globally. For partnership enquiries, reach out to us directly via the contact form above.
Do you offer pastoral care or personal support?
expand_more
Yes. We offer pastoral support and prayer to members of our community. Submit a prayer request using the form on this page, or reach out via the contact form selecting Pastoral Support as your subject. All conversations are treated with confidentiality, care, and prayer.
How can my church or organisation partner with KAG?
expand_more
KAG functions as a translocal apostolic network. We partner with aligned leaders, churches, fellowships, and hubs who carry the same vision of raising Kingdom Aligners. If you are interested in hosting an Impartation Night, joining the School of Alignment, or establishing a Kingdom Alignment Hub in your region, contact us through the form above.
