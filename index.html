About Our Ministry

A Movement of Faith & Purpose

Kingdom Alignment Global is a prophetic and apostolic movement raised to cultivate divine alignment in people, ministries, and communities. We exist to host God's presence, align His people with His purpose, and demonstrate His Kingdom through worship, teaching, prophetic impartation, and apostolic equipping.

KAG is not confined to a building; it is a living, mobile expression of God's Kingdom. We are sent to illuminate lives, realign hearts, and restore spiritual order wherever God places us.

"Our mission is to host God's presence through worship, teaching, and intercession"
